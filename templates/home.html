Welcome to the Scottish Garden Bird Identifier App!

This is a toy app to identify birds commonly seen in Scottish gardens.
To use the app, simply upload a picture of a bird (or birds!) and click 'Upload'.
Note that the app is only programmed to recognize certain bird species, so if you upload a picture of a bird not in the pre-trained model, it won't be recognized!
